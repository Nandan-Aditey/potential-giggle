About Us

We build tools that make research more discoverable, connected, and understandable without hiding knowledge behind paywalls.

Our Mission

Academic research is fragmented across papers, fields, and decades. Our mission is to organize knowledge into intuitive, interactive structures that help researchers, students, and curious minds explore ideas rather than just documents.

We believe understanding emerges from connections between papers, methods, theories, and results.

Core Principles

  • • Research focused
  • • Visual clarity over complexity
  • • Tooling that respects academic rigor
  • • Open access and transparency

How We Are Different

Graph-Based Understanding

Instead of static lists or feeds, we represent research as an interconnected graph making relationships, influences, and clusters immediately visible.

Human-Readable Summaries

Every paper is distilled into structured, readable explanations that focus on ideas, assumptions, and implications. We prevent you from wasting time going through hundreds of papers just to figure out the solution doesn't work.

No Paywalls, No Lock-In

We are fully open-source and do not monetize access to knowledge. Your learning is never gated behind subscriptions.

Open Source & Community-Driven

This project is free, open-source, and built for the community. We do not sell data, run ads, or prioritize growth over integrity. Contributions, feedback, and forks are welcome — the platform improves as the community grows.

Support Us

If you find value in open research tools, consider supporting organizations that keep knowledge freely accessible for everyone. We encourage contributing to one of the world’s most important open knowledge projects.
