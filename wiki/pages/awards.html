Gold Medal

We are proud to have achieved Gold Medal status! This milestone reflects our team's comprehensive success across all iGEM criteria. Our path to Gold was defined by our excellence in three key pillars: Best Integrated Human Practices (IHP), Best Sustainable Development Impact (SDG), and Best Model.

Best Sustainable Development Impact

Nominee

Our project was nominated for the Best Sustainable Development Impact award! ARGUS-2440 directly tackles SDG 2 (Zero Hunger), SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being), SDG 6 (Clean Water), SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure), SDG 12 (Responsible Consumption and Production) and SDG 14 (Life Below Water) by preventing nitrate leaching and restoring soil health.
